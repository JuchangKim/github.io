HireHub (React + Node.js + MongoDB)

HireHub is a comprehensive job-hunting and career-growth platform, connecting job seekers with top businesses. It features secure authentication, real-time search filters, and a modern UI for job postings, company reviews, and more.

Features

  • Posting & editing job postings (CRUD via REST API)
  • Secure user auth with JWT
  • Real-time search filters (useState in React)
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • News reading & commenting
  • Company reviews & job preference filters
